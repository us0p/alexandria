# Think critically
Ideas to help you focus on what really matters
## 80/20 rule
20% of inputs yield 80% of results. Focus on what matters.
## Strong beliefs, loosely held
Form well-reasoned opinions, but stay open to changing your mind with new evidence.
## Feynman technique
Teach a concept simply to uncover gaps in your understanding.
## Second-Order thinking
Anticipate long-term effects and unintended consequences of decisions.
## Inversion
Think in reverse: What could go wrong? What could cause failure?
## Probabilistic thinking
Evaluate the odds and outcomes instead of seeking certainty.
## Opportunity cost
Every decision is a trade off, recognize what you're giving up.
## Steel Manning
Strengthen opposing views to understand them deeply and clarify your own stance.
